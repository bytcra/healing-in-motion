A Few Words About COACH SHELBY

I'm Functioning for Almost 0 Years

Hi, I’m Shelby! As a certified NASM Personal Trainer and Nutrition Coach with a B.S. in Exercise Science, I’m on a mission to help women take control of their health and feel empowered in their bodies.

My journey has had many ups and downs — I’ve struggled with anxiety, endometriosis, and emotional eating, and I know firsthand how overwhelming it can feel to prioritize your health.

But through determination, self-compassion, and holistic strategies, I’ve overcome these obstacles and successfully lost 30 pounds along the way.
